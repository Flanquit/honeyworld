supporter’s page

thank you :)

dear indiegogo supporters

In December 2013, a few months after The Milk & Honey Distillery registered at the Israel Corporations Authority, we launched a crowdfunding campaign in Indiegogo. By pre-selling our first batch that will be distilled in our new equipment, we had hoped to raise $50,000 that would have enabled us to purchase ingredients and barrels for the first distillation run. Thanks to supporters from all over the world we raised no less than $65,000. We’d like to thank each and every one of you for your faith, trust and patience. You are a part of our story and we are look forward to the day you’ll receive your bottles.

 

Yours,

The Milk & Honey Team
