VISIT OUR
DISTILLERY

 

A TEL AVIVIAN EXPERIENCE LIKE NO OTHER

The Milk & Honey Distillery is a hidden gem among the workshops and warehouses of the small industrial zone on the border of Tel Aviv and Jaffa, right by the Bloomfield Soccer Stadium. Whisky-making is a special process that involves all senses. Moreover, it requires craftsmanship that is soaked with history, combining science, technology and handwork. We want to share the experience, and offer distillery tours for individuals and groups.

We also offer private and group tours for up to 35 guests, upon prior arrangement.

In our 1 hour tour, you will learn about the history and process of distillation, walk through the various stages of spirit making – from grain to glass – and finish with a guided tasting of our creations.

 

The distillery is easy to reach by public transportation and by car, with plenty of parking nearby. The visitor center is also available for private events –  please call ahead.

frequently asked questions

I’m interested in joining a public tour. Where can I see what tours you have, and when?

Right here!

How do I sign up for a tour?

On our guided tours page, click on the tour that you’re interested in joining. There you can choose the number of people, a date and time. At the bottom of the page, click BOOK NOW to book your tickets!

 
When do the distillery tours take place?

We offer tours throughout the week. Advanced booking is required. You can see all date tours and times for different tours here.

Can I come for a tour without booking ahead?

Luckily for us, we’re so busy we can’t accept walk-ins, and advanced booking is required. You can book a tour here.

How long does a visit take?

Depends on the tour! Our Wonderful World of Whisky tour is an hour long.

How much does a tour cost?

Costs vary on the tour. You can see prices here.

I’d like to come in just for a tasting, without a tour of the distillery. Is this possible?

We recommend coming for the full experience, but if you’d like to come just for a tasting, you can book a half-hour tasting session for up to 10 people for 250 NIS total. Advanced booking is required.

If you are interested in this, kindly be in touch at +972-3-6320491 or email us at mh@mh-distillery.com to book a tasting.

I’d like to come in just to buy some products, without a tasting or tour. Is this possible?

We recommend coming in for the full experience, but if you’d like to just buy our products, you have two options:

Check out the stores that sell our products within Israel here. We recommend calling ahead to see if the store is in stock and confirm their hours. You can also buy our products at Duty Free.

If you’d like to come to the distillery to buy our products, we ask that you call ahead to confirm your visit so that we can ensure someone will be around to help.

I bought a ticket but I can’t come anymore. How do I cancel my booking?

That’s too bad! First of all, we’d be happy to move your booking to another date (if relevant).

If you’d like to cancel, kindly be in touch with us at +972-3-6320491 or email us at mh@mh-distillery.com with the following information: credit card number, expiration date, CVV, and TZ/SSN/passport number (whichever was provided at time of purchase).

However, we cannot refund your money at the last minute. Kindly check our Cancellation Policy here.

Is your distillery wheelchair accessible?

Of course! Kindly inform us in advance if you will need wheelchair accessibility.

I’d like to make it a family visit - can I bring my kids?

We wholeheartedly believe that a visit at a whisky distillery is a fun and educational experience, but – it’s less fitting for kids under 18. During the tour, we walk around a working factory, so wandering hands and feet aren’t allowed! If there’s no option for a babysitter, kindly make sure everyone understands that we need them to be at their best behavior.

I’m pregnant and won’t be joining the tastings. Do you have a discount?

Yes! Please get in touch with us at mh@mh-distillery.com for a discount code.

I’m interested in booking a private tour for a small group. How do I do that?

Feel free to call us at +972-3-6320491 or email us at mh@mh-distillery.com to book a private tour. Advanced booking is required.

Private tours are available from morning to night, Sunday through Friday afternoon, provided prior availability.

I’m interested in booking a tour / planning an event for a group / my company / our team. How do I do that?

You can check out the available experiences here. Once you have an idea of what we can offer, feel free to call us at +972-3-6320491 or email us at mh@mh-distillery.com to start the planning process!

Private tours and events are available from morning to night, Sunday through Friday afternoon, provided prior availability.

I’d like to plan an event with catering / a meal / etc. Is this possible?

Definitely! We have a number of caterers and chefs we work with, and you can decide from a number of options. All our suppliers are kosher.

If you’re interested in bringing your own choice in catering, that’s no problem! We just will need to make sure they are also kosher, and need the possibility of figuring out any logistical needs before the event.

What’s your address? How do we find the distillery? Where can we park if we’re driving?

We are located at HatHiya 16, Tel Aviv. We recommend using your preferred maps app (Google Maps, Waze, Moovit) to find your way to us!

When you come to our address, look for a black door with yellow handles. There’s also a wooden sign that says M&H on it. If the door is closed, just ring the doorbell and someone will let you right in.

For parking, you can put into your app one of the following parking lots: Hazerem 12 Parking, Hayesod Parking Lot, 3429 Rd Parking Lot. All of those are a few minutes’ walk from us.

Are you kosher?

Yes! We are approved kosher by the Tel Aviv Rabbinate. All our products as well as any catering at the distillery is kosher.

Where can I buy your products in Israel?

Check out the stores that sell our products within Israel here. We recommend calling ahead to see if the store is in stock and confirm their hours.

Where can I buy your products outside of Israel?

You can check out here if we currently distribute in your country.

If not, your best bet is either to buy our products on the Master of Malt website or to ask a friend to bring you a bottle from Israel.

We currently do not ship abroad.
