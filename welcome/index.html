WHO WE ARE

The story of Zimbabwe is that of a former agriculture giant that has dropped from being one of the main exporters of agricultural products to a nation depending on hand-outs given by aid organisations. The past two decades have seen the closure of many companies which has left the country with one of the highest unemployment rates on the African continent. Faced with this situation, the country has lost most of its economically active young men and women as they flee to neighbouring countries in search of income to support their families
